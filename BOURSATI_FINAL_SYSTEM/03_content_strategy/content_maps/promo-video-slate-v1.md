# Promo Video Slate V1

This slate defines ten first-draft vertical promo videos. They use the approved Kinetic Kuwait Editorial direction, safe app recordings, existing campaign assets, and App Store-only calls to action.

## Videos

| Video | Target | Message | Direction |
|---|---|---|---|
| `promo-01-launch-hero` | General launch audience | بورصة الكويت في جيبك | Hero launch film with cinematic motion and app proof. |
| `promo-02-disclosure-alert` | Users who care about official disclosures | من التنبيه إلى الإفصاح | Safe notification recording, alert-to-source proof. |
| `promo-03-market-morning` | Daily market followers | ابدأ يومك من ملخص السوق | Morning routine and market-summary habit. |
| `promo-04-news-vs-disclosure` | Educational audience | الخبر عنوان، الإفصاح مصدر | Carousel-style educational explainer. |
| `promo-05-company-context` | Users researching companies | افتح الشركة واقرأ المسار كامل | Company reading map with light product proof. |
| `promo-06-portfolio-clarity` | Portfolio users | محفظتك أوضح | Portfolio organization and clarity. |
| `promo-07-watchlist-habit` | Regular company followers | خل الشركات المهمة قريبة | Watchlist and alerts habit. |
| `promo-08-kuwait-editorial-brand` | Brand awareness | محلي، واضح، حديث | Pure editorial brand energy with Kuwait visual language. |
| `promo-09-app-proof-fast-cut` | App Store conversion audience | شاهد التطبيق الحقيقي | Mostly real app recordings. |
| `promo-10-bumper-pack` | Ads and story reminders | حمّل بورصتي من App Store | Six-second CTA bumper. |
