# Content Strategy

This folder turns the final marketing plan into captions, posting order, calendars, and asset references.

The strategy source of truth is:

`../02_marketing_plan/boursati-final-marketing-plan.md`

## Folder Roles

- `week_1_launch_execution/` - active Week 1 posting order, captions, checklist, and Instagram profile setup.
- `posting_plan/` - practical launch and rotation plan.
- `captions/` - final launch captions and reusable caption banks.
- `calendar/` - rotation calendar for later scheduling.
- `content_maps/` - asset inventory, carousel plans, video slate, and review tables.
- `review_workbooks/` - spreadsheet review files.

## Operating Rule

Do not let asset volume drive the message.

Every post should support the core promise:

> بورصة الكويت بوضوح، من مكان واحد.

Use practical product clarity first. Use abstract/editorial lines only after the viewer understands what Boursati does.
