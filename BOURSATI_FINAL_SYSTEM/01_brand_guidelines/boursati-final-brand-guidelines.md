# Boursati Final Brand Guidelines

## Source Of Truth

This folder represents the approved Boursati marketing direction: Kinetic Kuwait Editorial. Older explorations are archived and should not be used as production references unless intentionally revived.

## Brand Idea

Boursati makes following Boursa Kuwait feel clearer, closer, and more organized from an Arabic-first iOS app.

The visual world should feel energetic and modern, but still credible. It should not feel like generic corporate fintech, and it should not rely on app screenshots in every post.

## Visual Direction

- High-contrast black, paper, white, and vivid Boursati blue.
- Kinetic blue strokes, editorial grids, sharp composition, and strong Arabic typography.
- White line illustrations inspired by Kuwait culture and market context.
- Kuwait Towers illustration must show the three-tower structure with the correct spires and sphere arrangement.
- Screenshots are used only when the post is proving a real app feature.
- Phone mockups must sit in a clean visual zone with no illustration or stroke overlapping the device.

## Color System

- Boursati Blue: `#1148FF`
- Support Blue: `#578AFF`
- Deep Black: `#0A0B0D`
- Paper: `#F4F0E7`
- Soft White: `#F8F8F3`
- Ink: `#090A0F`

## Tone

Arabic-first, clear, confident, and practical. Kuwaiti dialect is allowed when it makes the message feel natural, but it should not become casual to the point of weakening trust.

Avoid investment advice, predictions, performance promises, and buy/sell language.

## Content Pillars

- Market clarity
- Disclosure awareness
- Company context
- Portfolio organization
- Daily learning
- Launch awareness

## CTA Rule

Use App Store only:

`حمّل بورصتي من App Store`

Do not mention Android, Google Play, subscriptions, trials, or paid plans.
