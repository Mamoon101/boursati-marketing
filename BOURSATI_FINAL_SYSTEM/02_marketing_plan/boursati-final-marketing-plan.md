# Boursati Final Marketing Plan

## Strategy In One Line

بورصة الكويت بوضوح، من مكان واحد.

Boursati helps people in Kuwait follow Boursa Kuwait clearly, in Arabic, from one organized iPhone app.

## Current Phase

Launch and early-account building.

The goal is not to show every asset we have. The goal is to make a new viewer understand Boursati quickly, trust the tone, and know why the app is useful.

## Audience

People in Kuwait who follow Boursa Kuwait and want a clearer mobile experience for market context, disclosures, news, alerts, summaries, and portfolio review.

They are not hype traders. They are serious retail investors who check the market during the day and want less scattered information.

## Audience Problem

Today, following Boursa Kuwait can feel scattered:

- official sources for disclosures and PDFs,
- brokerage screens for holdings,
- generic finance apps for prices,
- news feeds for headlines,
- Excel or notes for portfolio tracking.

Boursati's job is to make that follow-up feel organized from one Arabic mobile experience.

## Positioning

Boursati is not a trading app and not an investment advisor.

It is the Kuwait-native market follow-up app: clear market picture, company context, official disclosures, alerts, summaries, and portfolio visibility in one place.

## Message Hierarchy

Use this order before writing captions, posts, carousels, or video scripts:

1. Core promise: follow Boursa Kuwait clearly, in Arabic, from one place.
2. Feature proof: prices, charts, news, disclosures, company pages, alerts, summaries, portfolio.
3. Hero behavior: from push notification to official disclosure quickly.
4. Daily habit: start from the market picture, then go deeper.
5. Brand flavor: editorial lines and poetic phrasing only after the practical value is clear.

Primary Arabic line:

> بورصة الكويت بوضوح، من مكان واحد.

Useful supporting lines:

- تابع السوق بدون لويه.
- من التنبيه إلى الإفصاح مباشرة.
- ابدأ من ملخص السوق، ثم ادخل في التفاصيل.
- الشركات، الأخبار، الإفصاحات، ومحفظتك ضمن تجربة واحدة.

Use abstract lines such as `كل مسار له مكان` only as secondary support, not as the first explanation of the product.

## Launch Pillars

### 1. Market Clarity

Explain that Boursati gives users a clearer starting point for Boursa Kuwait: market status, movement, gainers/losers, summaries, and company paths.

### 2. Disclosure Awareness

Make the disclosure feature a signature behavior. The strongest story is simple: notification arrives, user opens it, user reaches the official disclosure.

### 3. Company Context

Show that company pages connect price, charts, news, disclosures, and data. This helps a user move from headline to context.

### 4. Portfolio Organization

Position portfolio tracking as calm review, not performance hype. The message is organization and visibility, not returns.

### 5. Daily Learning

Use tips and simple educational posts to make the account useful beyond promotion.

## Channel Roles

- Instagram grid: first impression and brand identity.
- Instagram feed: clear product promise, feature proof, and educational posts.
- Instagram stories: quick reminders, daily prompts, and disclosure/market habits.
- Reels/TikTok: short educational hooks and app-behavior stories.
- X: concise market-context statements, launch updates, and disclosure-first messaging.

## Visual Direction

The default visual system is dark Kinetic Kuwait Editorial:

- dark ink background,
- electric Boursati blue,
- bold Arabic headlines,
- Kuwait-inspired white line illustrations,
- strong grid structure,
- precise App Store CTA placement.

Light/off-white designs are secondary campaign variation. Do not let them replace the dark identity.

Use app screenshots only when they prove a real feature or workflow. Do not force a phone mockup into every post.

## Caption Rules

Keep captions practical and direct:

1. Say what the app helps with.
2. Explain the user benefit in one sentence.
3. Close with the App Store CTA when appropriate.

Avoid long caption blocks during launch. If the post itself is abstract, the caption must be functional.

## Safety Rules

- No buy, sell, hold, or stock-picking recommendations.
- No promised returns or predictive language.
- No fake market numbers.
- No "best app" claims.
- No subscription, trial, premium, Android, or Google Play language unless explicitly approved.
- Use real screenshots only when contextually useful and free of private information.
- Keep all CTAs App Store-only:

`حمّل بورصتي من App Store`

## What Success Looks Like

A new viewer should understand within seconds:

- this is for Boursa Kuwait,
- it is Arabic-first,
- it helps organize market follow-up,
- disclosures and company context are core,
- it is available on the App Store.
