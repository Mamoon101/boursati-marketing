# Boursati Final Marketing Plan

## Current Phase

Pre-launch asset preparation while posting is postponed until the next app update is ready. The goal is to build enough approved assets to post consistently without rushing new creative production.

## Launch Goal

Introduce Boursati as the Arabic-first way to follow Boursa Kuwait through clear market summaries, company pages, disclosures, news, watchlists, and portfolio context.

## Audience

People in Kuwait who follow Boursa Kuwait and want a clearer mobile experience for market context, disclosures, news, and portfolio review.

## Messaging Priorities

1. Boursa Kuwait in your pocket.
2. Start from the market summary, then go deeper.
3. Disclosures are the official source.
4. Company pages bring price, news, disclosures, and data into one path.
5. Portfolio review should feel organized, not scattered.
6. Boursati is available on the App Store.

## Channel Use

- Instagram grid: launch identity and brand story.
- Instagram feed: feature-led and educational posts.
- Instagram stories: quick reminders, launch prompts, and daily tips.
- Reels/TikTok covers: vertical educational hooks.
- X banners: crisp market-context statements and launch headers.

## Posting System

Use the launch batch first, then rotate through platform static assets and educational bank posts. Captions live in the content strategy folder and should be reviewed manually before posting.

## Safety Rules

- No recommendations to buy, sell, hold, or choose a stock.
- No promised returns or predictive language.
- No fake market numbers.
- Use real screenshots only when contextually useful.
- Keep all CTAs App Store-only.
