# Boursati Digital Assets

Reusable brand assets for Boursati marketing exports.

## App Icon

Marketing copy:

- `assets/app-icon-1024.png`

Source of truth in the app repo:

- `../boursa-kuwait/ios-native/Boursati/Resources/Assets.xcassets/AppIcon.appiconset/icon-1024.png`

Use this icon in rendered social posts, video end cards, thumbnails, and launch assets. Do not use generated or recreated logo marks when the official app icon can be used.

## App Store Badge

Marketing copy:

- `assets/app-store-badge.svg`

Source:

- [Apple Marketing Tools badge asset](https://developer.apple.com/assets/elements/badges/download-on-the-app-store.svg)

Use this only while Boursati is iOS-only. Do not add Google Play or Android CTAs until the product truth changes.

## Boursati URL QR

Marketing copy:

- `assets/boursati-qr.svg`

Destination:

- `https://boursati.app`

Use this mostly in story-format assets, end cards, posters, or offline/event material where scanning is useful.

## Phone Frame

Marketing copy:

- `assets/phone-frame.svg`

Use this as a lightweight framing asset when the screenshot needs a device boundary. Keep real app screenshots as the center of the visual.
